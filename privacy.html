Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to YT Automation ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop application that helps automate YouTube content creation and uploading.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • YouTube Account Information: When you connect your YouTube account, we access your account credentials through Google's OAuth system
  • API Keys: Gemini AI API keys you provide for content generation features
  • Video Content: Videos and metadata you choose to process through our application
  • Settings and Preferences: Your application settings, upload preferences, and configuration data

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Application Usage: Information about how you use the application, including features accessed and errors encountered
  • System Information: Basic system information necessary for application functionality
  • Log Data: Application logs that help us diagnose issues and improve performance

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our application services
  • Process and upload your videos to YouTube
  • Generate AI-powered content using your provided API keys
  • Authenticate your YouTube accounts securely
  • Improve application functionality and user experience
  • Provide technical support and troubleshooting
  • Ensure application security and prevent misuse

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google/YouTube API: For uploading videos and managing YouTube accounts
  • Google Gemini AI: For AI-powered content generation (using your API keys)
  • Social Media Platforms: For downloading content from Instagram, TikTok, and other platforms

4.2 Data Sharing Limitations

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and the safety of our users
  • In connection with a business transfer or merger

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Application settings and preferences
  • YouTube authentication tokens
  • API keys and credentials
  • Video processing cache

5.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data stored locally
  • Secure API communication using HTTPS
  • OAuth 2.0 for YouTube authentication
  • Regular security updates and patches

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information stored in the application
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the application
  • Revoke YouTube account access at any time
  • Remove API keys and stop AI content generation

6.2 Data Portability

You can export your settings and preferences from the application at any time. Your videos and content remain under your control on your local device and connected platforms.

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own when using third-party services like Google APIs. These transfers are conducted in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the application
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing notice through the application interface for significant changes

10. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Clearing application data through settings
  • Uninstalling the application
  • Revoking API access permissions

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@ytautomation.com

Support: Through the application's help section

We will respond to your inquiries within 30 days of receipt.

12. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Google API Services User Data Policy
  • YouTube Terms of Service
